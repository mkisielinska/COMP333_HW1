Who are we? What do we believe?

We're a couple of cool cats who think that sharing and rating music among friends is the best way to expand your musical horizons. Arguably, there is no better way to become a musical snob than with us!

How does this work?

You sign up and are then instantly able to share playlists, rate songs, and listen to music from all different streaming platforms with us. We let you connect with your musical idols through webinars with an open Q&A twice a month, you can write your own articles where you hail or trasg new artists. It's musical journalism made accessible and fun. We have all the features of all of the current platforms you may be using, and more! because of the community we foster.
